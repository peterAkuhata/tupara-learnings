# Tupara School

A collection of interactive learning tools to help Tupara with his school lessons.

## Contents

### Science Quiz

[science_quiz.html](science_quiz.html) — An interactive Year 9 Science Quiz with 104 multi-select questions across 15 topics:

- Lab safety & hazards
- Lab equipment
- Cells & cell structure
- The periodic table
- Forces & motion
- Electricity & circuits
- Chemical reactions
- Acids, alkalis & pH
- States of matter
- Reproduction & genetics
- Light & sound
- Human body systems
- Energy
- Ecology & ecosystems
- Earth & space

Open the file in a browser to start the quiz. It includes real-time progress tracking, score calculation, and visual feedback on answers.
