# Tupara's Learning Hub

A collection of interactive learning tools to help Tupara with his school lessons.

**[Open the Learning Hub](https://peterAkuhata.github.io/tupara-learnings/)**

## Contents

### Year 9 Science

Interactive lessons and a 104-question quiz across 15 topics including lab safety, cells, the periodic table, forces, electricity, chemical reactions, acids & pH, states of matter, reproduction & genetics, light & sound, human body systems, energy, ecology, and Earth & space.

### Prime Numbers

Interactive lessons and a 20-question quiz covering identifying primes, prime factor decomposition, powers of primes, HCF & LCM, and simplifying fractions.
